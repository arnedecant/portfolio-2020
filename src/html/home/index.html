About

My name is Arne Decant and I was born and raised in Kortrijk, Belgium. I went to school to study a joined education accounting/informatics, whereafter I chose to go to college and continue in informatics. I studied MCT (Multimedia and Communication Technology) at Howest, University College of West Flanders, and finished my education in June 2014.

I wrote my first line of code in 2009, when I was 15, but only learned how to do so properly 2 years after, during my first year in college. In 2014 I started my first job as a frontend webdeveloper and worked on several websites and applications for the local police departments and clients in the event sector. In 2018 I started at Nanopixel, an award-winning 3D visualisations company located in Roeselare. There, I was product owner and lead developer of Showreal, working for clients like Versluys, Degroote, ION, Vestio and many more from the real estate market.
